The Deep Now

During Flow we may experience:

Selflessness - Our brains switch into a different gear, which shuts down "self-criticism" as we are fully immersed in our project

Timelessness - Those experiences of time dilation - "The time flew by", or time distortion - dodging bullets in the Matrix!

Effortless - "Effortless effort" where it feels like our body is completely on our side

Richness - From the macro:- profound life changing moments, to the micro:- being in your element


This can happen collectively too - have you ever seen a team, when they really click, or felt an electric atmosphere during a performance ?
